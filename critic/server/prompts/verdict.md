
## Verdict

At the end of your review, you MUST include this section:

**Verdict**: Request full manuscript / Pass / Pass with reservations
**Weakest passages**: Quote the 3 weakest sentences in the manuscript and explain why they fail.
**Strongest passages**: Quote the 3 strongest sentences and explain why they work.
**Best**: At its best (where?), what is the quality level of the manuscript?
**Worst**: At its worse (where?), what is the quality level of the manuscript?
**One-line pitch test**: If you had to sell this to an editor in one sentence, what would you say? If you can't formulate one, say so and explain why.
